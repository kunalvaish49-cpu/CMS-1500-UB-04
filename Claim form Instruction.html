CMS-1500 Overview

CMS-1500 Form

The standard claim form used by non-institutional providers and suppliers to bill Medicare Part B, Medicaid, and most other payers for professional services rendered by physicians and other qualified healthcare professionals.

Patient & Insurance Information — Boxes 1–13
Box # Field Name Description Reference Image
Box 1
Insurance TypeRequired
Indicates the type of health insurance plan and policy (Medicare, Medicaid, TRICARE, CHAMPVA, Group Health Plan, FECA, or Other). Directs how the claim will be processed.
Box 1a
Insured ID NumberRequired
Patient's policy number, group, or identification number as assigned by the payer. Also called Subscriber ID or Member ID.
Box 2
Patient NameRequired
Full legal name of the patient (last, first, middle initial). Must match the name the payer has on file — do not enter a preferred name here.
Box 3
Patient Birthdate & SexRequired
Patient's date of birth (MM/DD/YYYY) and gender on record with the insurance company.
Box 4
Insured NameConditional
Name of the policyholder if different from the patient.
Box 5

Full mailing address including city, state, and ZIP code for the patient.
Box 6
Patient Relationship to InsuredRequired
Indicates whether the patient is the insured (self), spouse, child, or other relative of the insured.
Box 7
Insured AddressConditional
Complete only if the address of the insured is different from the patient's address.

Box 20
Outside Lab? Charges?Required
Indicates if an outside lab was used and the charges. Mark NO if labs were completed in-house.
Box 21
Diagnosis / Nature of Illness or InjuryRequired
ICD-10 (or ICD-9 legacy) diagnosis codes for the encounter. May vary from other diagnoses the patient has on record.
Box 22

Procedures & Charges — Boxes 24a–24j
Box 24a
Date(s) of ServiceRequired
Start and end dates of service. In many cases start and end dates will be the same.
Box 24b
Place of ServiceRequired
Two-digit code for service location (office, hospital, telehealth, etc.).
Box 24c
EMG (Emergency Indicator)Optional
Indicates if services were an emergency. Generally left blank.
Box 24d
Procedures, Services, or SuppliesRequired
CPT/HCPCS codes describing services provided (e.g., 90837, 99204). Modifiers are also entered here.
Box 24e
Diagnosis PointerRequired
Linked to the diagnosis code in Box 21. Enter the alpha-numeric identifier from Box 21 — not the actual diagnosis code.
Box 24f
ChargesRequired
Billed charge for each service line to the payer.
Box 24g
Days or UnitsRequired
Number of services, time units, or dosage.
Box 24h
EPSDT / Family PlanOptional
Indicates Early & Periodic Screening, Diagnosis, Treatment (EPSDT) or family planning. Often left blank.
Box 24i
ID QualifierOptional
Legacy qualifier for Box 24j. Generally left blank or filled with "ZZ" to indicate NPI is submitted in Box 24j.
Box 24j
Rendering Provider NPIRequired
10-digit NPI of the individual rendering provider.

UB-04 Overview

UB-04 Form

The uniform institutional claim form used by hospitals, skilled nursing facilities, and other institutional providers to bill for inpatient, outpatient, and other facility-based services. Managed by the National Uniform Billing Committee (NUBC).
